Senior Traffic Growth Expert — SEO Middle Platform — Alibaba (China)

Aug 2017 – Sep 2019

Built a global e-commerce SEO middle platform supporting AliExpress, Alibaba.com, Taobao Overseas, and other global businesses for organic growth — covering hreflang, canonicalization, internal-linking, and schema at multi-country scale.

  • The diagnosis most teams miss. AliExpress and Alibaba.com had ~5–6 billion indexed pages each (per GSC / index coverage). Counting only the pages that actually get organic search traffic, only ~2M of those 5–6B (~0.03–0.04%) earned their existence — the rest were diluting crawl budget and drowning out the good content. Previous long-tail SEO work had added pages, which made the problem worse.
  • Phase 1 — content convergence. On AliExpress, buyer-side and seller-side product detail pages were both indexed. Removed ~10–20M duplicate landing pages. Long-tail non-brand organic traffic went up ~70K UV/day in ~10 days with no new content. Sometimes the answer to "more traffic" is "fewer pages."
  • Phase 2 — the SEO middle platform. Built shared infrastructure — hreflang, canonicalization, schema, sitemaps, internal linking at platform scale — for AliExpress, Alibaba.com, and Taobao Overseas, plus a keyword-clustering pipeline for ~600M keywords across ~10–15 languages. DAMO Academy quoted 20 engineers × 1 year per language to do the clustering; my team did it with 1 engineer × 1 month (~50% coverage, using Google SERP as a clustering reference). The principle generalized: stop building parallel per-market systems; build one platform that does it right and every site benefits.
  • Results. Reversed a 3–4-year organic-traffic decline with +10–15% YoY total site UV in year 1; orders (AliExpress) and quote requests (Alibaba.com) growing in parallel. Taobao Overseas long-tail added ~150K UV/day. Saved 90%+ headcount vs. DAMO Academy's quoted cost (conservative; the real saving is 99%+). Full-funnel UX optimization — a separate but related project — lifted organic CR by 10%.
