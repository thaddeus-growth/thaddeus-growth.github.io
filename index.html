Senior Traffic Growth Expert — SEO Middle Platform — Alibaba (China)

Aug 2017 – Sep 2019

Built a global e-commerce SEO middle platform supporting AliExpress, Alibaba.com, Taobao Overseas, and other global businesses for organic growth — covering hreflang, canonicalization, internal-linking, and schema at multi-country scale.

  • The diagnosis most teams miss. AliExpress and Alibaba.com had ~5–6 billion indexed pages each. Counting only the pages that actually drive business outcomes (orders on AliExpress, quote requests on Alibaba.com — B2B has no orders), only ~0.03–0.04% earned their existence — the rest were diluting crawl budget and drowning out the good content. Previous long-tail SEO work had added pages, which made the problem worse.
  • Phase 1 — content convergence. On AliExpress, buyer-side and seller-side product detail pages were both indexed. Removed the redundant set. Long-tail organic traffic went up ~70K UV/day almost immediately — a 7× lift on the metric that mattered, with no new content. Sometimes the answer to "more traffic" is "fewer pages."
  • Phase 2 — the SEO middle platform. Built shared infrastructure — hreflang, canonicalization, schema, sitemaps, internal linking at platform scale — for AliExpress, Alibaba.com, and Taobao Overseas. The principle generalized: stop building parallel per-market systems; build one platform that does it right and every site benefits.
  • Results. Reversed multi-year organic-traffic decline. Long-tail non-brand organic on AliExpress / Alibaba.com went from < 1,000 UV/day to ~200,000 UV/day. Taobao Overseas long-tail added ~150K UV/day. Saved 90%+ headcount vs. the per-market, per-team model. Full-funnel UX optimization lifted organic conversion rate by 10%.
